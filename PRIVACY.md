# Privacy Policy for Tab Master

**Last updated: 2024**

## Overview

Tab Master ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Tab Master handles information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Information We Collect

**Tab Master does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

## Data Usage

### Local Data Storage

Tab Master uses Chrome's built-in storage APIs to store:

- Your tab groups and custom groups
- Recently closed tabs history
- Your preferences and settings

**All data is stored locally on your device.**

### No Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

We do not share any information with third parties.

## Permissions Used

Tab Master requires the following Chrome permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `tabs` | Read tab information (title, URL, favicon) to display in the new tab page |
| `storage` | Save your groups, settings, and recent tabs locally |

## Data Security

All data processed by Tab Master stays on your device. We implement standard security practices to protect your local data.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the updated version here with a revised "Last updated" date.
